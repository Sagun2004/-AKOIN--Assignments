About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.


Working in partnership with leading UK universities and industry organisations, IDR combines academic insight with real-world projects, ensuring practitioners develop the skills needed to manage modern technology and AI risks.

Our Model

Academy

Bootcamps and structured training programs for students and professionals building careers in digital risk, cyber governance, and AI assurance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new intellectual property, governance models and frameworks for emerging challenges such as AI risk, algorithmic accountability and digital resilience.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations to implement global frameworks including NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 across financial services, critical infrastructure and regulated sectors.

Who We Serve

IDR supports a growing community of students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners seeking to develop expertise in digital, cyber and AI risk.


Our programmes support professionals across sectors including financial services, government, critical infrastructure, technology firms and regulated industries.
